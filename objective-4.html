The objective can be accessed directly via this link:
https://git.kringlecastle.com/Upatree/santas_castle_automation

Navigate to https://git.kringlecastle.com/Upatree/santas_castle_automation and click "Find file". Search for 'zip' and download the file 'ventilation_diagram.zip'

Click "Commits", scroll down to "important update" and click on the entry. The 'ventilation_diagram.zip' password is revealed to be 'Yippee-ki-yay'
